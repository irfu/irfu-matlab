
QLI = QuickLooks IRFU ("IRFU quicklooks")

SolO quicklooks generated at IRFU.

NOTE: Not to be confused with LESIA's summary plots or BIA_QL3.
NOTE: These quicklooks use SolO/RPW datasets AND datasets from other instruments.


For an example of how to generate quicklooks locally, look at
    generate_quicklooks_demo.m

To edit the quicklooks, check files
    quicklooks_24_6_2_h.m
    quicklooks_7days.m
